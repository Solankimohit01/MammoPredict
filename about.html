About MammoPredict

MammoPredict is an AI-powered web application designed to assist with early breast cancer detection and analysis. Our platform uses advanced machine learning models such as Random Forest and Support Vector Machines (SVM) to deliver predictive insights based on user reports and symptoms. Whether uploading test reports or describing symptoms, MammoPredict empowers users with early awareness and actionable health information.
